We're dedicated to improving Veteran lives.

These men and women stood up and were counted on when their Nation called them. There are various opportunities to volunteer in the VA hospitals in Bonham, Dallas, Waco, Temple and San Antonio throughout the year.

Get Involved

Volunteers are always appreciated. For more information on how you can support Vet to Vet ®, please contact us today.

Become a Sponsor

Show your support by donating through Mobile Giving or contact us for other ways to sponsor. Your contribution directly impacts the lives of disabled and unhoused veterans.

We have many options to participate.

  • play Bingo once a month at the VA hospitals we serve
  • help with the various BBQs during the year
  • contribute money, clothing, or gifts
  • assist with the annual patient festival during the second Thursday in October
  • help prepare Christmas dinners or delivering gifts to these men and women hospitalized during the holiday season

Vet to Vet ® is an independent non-profit organization 501c3 founded by Vets and made up of Veterans and Civilians to help Veterans. Vet to Vet ® is not affiliated or associated with the "operation of Vet2Vet" located at 24 Homewood Ave, North Providence, RI.
